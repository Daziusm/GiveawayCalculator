UNDERSTANDING DISCORD GIVEAWAYS

Most Discord giveaway bots use a random selection process, picking winners from users who have entered by clicking a reaction button or similar mechanism. This tool helps you understand your actual odds of winning based on the number of entrants and winners.

When there are requirements (like having a certain number of invites or messages), the pool of eligible participants decreases, which can significantly affect your odds.

HOW TO USE

  • Step 1: Enter the number of accounts you're entering with
  • Step 2: Enter the total number of participants in the giveaway
  • Step 3: Specify how many winners will be selected
  • Step 4: Choose the calculation type based on giveaway requirements
  • Step 5: Click "Calculate Odds" to see your chances of winning

ADVANCED USAGE

For giveaways with invite requirements:

  1. Select "With Invite Requirements" from the dropdown
  2. The calculator will estimate how many other participants meet the requirements
TIP: For the most accurate results, try to estimate the actual number of participants who meet all requirements, not just the total reaction count.
